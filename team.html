Team Mechanical Maniacs

Central Florida storytellers + engineers.

Mechanical Maniacs is a long-running FIRST LEGO League Challenge team based in Palm Bay, Florida. We blend robotics, archaeology, and community journalism so UNEARTHED artifacts feel alive.

Roles

Every Maniac stewards a story.

Our team pairs each artifact story with a student lead who coordinates interviews, robot missions, and exhibit materials.

What defines us

Our mentors got their start with the Seminole County Robotics Association more than a decade ago. Mechanical Maniacs has since earned Core Values and Presentation awards at Central Florida qualifiers, thanks to a culture that balances serious research with goofy prototype sessions.

How we collaborate

Each build session ends with a “story circle” where teammates present evidence from their dig site. Those insights guide coding priorities and outreach invites for the week.

  • Monday missions: robot run logs + attachment tweaks
  • Wednesday workshops: artifact documentation and interview prep
  • Friday share-outs: community event planning and presentation rehearsal
