UNEARTHED 2025

Digging up stories, safeguarding the future.

FIRST LEGO League challenges teams to uncover cultural artifacts, share who preserved them, and prototype technology that protects fragile histories. Our UNEARTHED plan blends storytelling with field-ready engineering.

Challenge focus

We framed our research question around Florida’s coastal communities: How can students help families capture stories about objects before hurricanes or development erase them?

  • Interview historians at the Museum of Seminole County History and Orlando Science Center archives
  • Prototype a portable photogrammetry station powered by LEGO SPIKE Prime sensors
  • Design workshops where families tag artifacts with QR codes linking to recorded oral histories
  • Create a digital exhibit where UNEARTHED visitors can explore 3D scans inside our artifact grid

Robot priorities

Volt Voyager, our UNEARTHED robot, tackles missions that align with the story theme.

  • Reliable excavation and transport runs with a low-profile scoop attachment
  • Color-sensed alignment for the catalog and restoration missions
  • Modular frame so we can swap between artifact placement and display walls quickly
