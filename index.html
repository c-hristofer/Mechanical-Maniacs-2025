Innovation Project

Connecting energy-saving habits to daily living.

Inspired by the 2024-2025 FIRST LEGO League Challenge theme, we designed a smart home companion that coaches families to reduce wasted power while keeping life fun and effortless.

Problem

Households struggle to visualize the impact of their energy use. Most tools are complicated, and busy families need playful reminders instead of more apps to manage.

Breakthrough Idea

Little Volts is building a tabletop assistant and mobile dashboard that celebrates savings with positive feedback, friendly characters, and weekly challenges tailored to each family.

Prototype

Our build combines LEGO Spike sensors, a microcontroller, and recycled materials. A color-changing voltage vine shows progress, and our app unlocks new sparks as energy goals are met.

Impact Goals

We aim to reduce participating families’ electricity usage by 15% over a season, create lesson kits for classrooms, and share our open-source designs with other FLL teams.

Robot Game

Precision driving. Adaptable strategy.

Our robot, Volt Runner, uses modular attachments and a color-coded calibration system to conquer missions efficiently.

  • Two-motor drivetrain with gyro assisted straight-line accuracy
  • Quick-swap mission attachments tuned to maximize combo runs
  • In-match diagnostics that help us adapt when the field changes
  • Practice logs reviewed weekly to iterate and improve consistency
